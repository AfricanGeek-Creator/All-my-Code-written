Resistance Training

This is a form of training that helps increase in muscular size or improve in muscle definition. The most common type of muscular hypertrophy occurs through weightlifting or weight training. Equipment or the use thereof would be to your advantage for this form of training. Weight training regimen involves moving items that offer resistance such as; Free weights (barbells, dumbbells, kettle bells) Weight machines (pulleys and stacks) Body weight (push ups and pull ups)

Hybrid - Fat loss Training

(Think of your body as an empty barbell -Bartram.) Body weight training requires one to get creative with their body as well as use it in the right way for these type of workouts. The advantage of using bodyweight exercises is that you are in control of your own body awareness. This form of training is usually an underrated mechanism of exercise because it requires a vast amount of energy and endurance. No equipments are needed for bodyweight training however, the use of resistant bands can be accompanied for the workouts, the pace of your training also increases. Exercises include a lot of plyometric movements (jumps).

Strength & Explosive Training

Explosive and strength training combines a variation of training methods into one program, to help increase your power, agility and mobility output. These drills and methods are often used by athletes who generate a quick burst of maximal effort. Though there is a slight risk in this training style, it is a great way maximize muscular strength for your body. Explosive exercises at their final level can also be referred to as ballistic movements.

Toning and Shaping

A very common type of training for people who are looking into losing weight or shred down on the fat percentage or muscle percentage they have gained. The trick about this program is understanding your body as a whole. There are different forms of body types, and based on your specific body type, we are able to design a program that will be generated to you. This form of training, like many others should be accompanied with a diet plan that will assist in your weight loss journey. The first mistake people make while following this method of training is that they starve themselves hoping to lose the percentage of fat or muscle gained. Keep in mind your body needs a portion of food types (macronutrients) to help you perform and give you the results you desire. Do not be like others, and make the same mistake.
